Afghanistan Tourist Visa Types

  • Single-entry tourist visa (usually valid for 30 days)
  • Multiple-entry tourist visa (validity depends on embassy/consulate approval)
  • Transit visa (for short-term passage through Afghanistan)
  • Visa on arrival (available for select nationalities at Hamid Karzai International Airport)
  • eVisa (available for citizens of specific countries via the official Afghan visa portal)

Note: Visa policies may vary depending on nationality. Check available options using the dropdown menu or consult your nearest Afghan embassy or consulate.

Overstay Fine: Tourists who overstay their visa may be subject to fines or restrictions on future visits.

Some types of tourist visas can be extended by applying through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs or the Afghan Immigration Department in Kabul.

Visa Requirements and Application

Each visa type has its own requirements for documents that need to be submitted; a few commonly needed documents include:

  • A recent passport photograph
  • A passport or travel document, valid for no less than 6 months
  • Valid health insurance
  • A national identity card for certain nationalities
  • A travel ticket showing onward journey (for transit visas, within 96 hours of arrival)
  • For GCC residents, a copy of your residence permit
  • For visa on arrival for Indian nationals:
    • Permanent residence card (Green Card) issued by the USA
    • Visit visa issued by the USA
    • Residence/visit visa issued by the UK or European Union countries

Once you have all your documents ready, you can apply either directly via the GDRFA website or through an authorised agency such as your travel agent, approved airlines, or your booked hotel. Processing time varies but usually takes about 48 hours.

For detailed and up-to-date information, please refer to the GDRFA official website or consult your travel agent.

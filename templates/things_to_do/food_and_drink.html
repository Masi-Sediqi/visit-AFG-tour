Unique Food & Drink Experiences in Afghanistan

  • Savor traditional Afghan dishes like Kabuli Pulao, Mantu (steamed dumplings), and Ashak (leek-filled dumplings) at local family kitchens and restaurants.
  • Taste freshly baked naan (Afghan bread) straight from the tandoor ovens, a staple in every meal.
  • Enjoy aromatic green tea or kahwa (Afghan spiced tea) served with cardamom and accompanied by sweet treats.
  • Experience lively food markets bursting with fresh fruits, nuts, spices, and dried fruits like mulberries and pomegranates.
  • Delight in qabili palau — Afghanistan’s national dish, featuring fragrant rice, tender meat, and caramelized carrots and raisins.
  • Sample doogh, a refreshing yogurt-based drink seasoned with mint, perfect for cooling off on warm days.
  • Indulge in sweet sheer chai (milk tea) flavored with cardamom and sugar, often enjoyed with friends and family.
  • Discover regional specialties from across Afghanistan’s diverse provinces, reflecting the country’s rich cultural mosaic.

Afghanistan invites food lovers to embark on a flavorful journey, where every meal is a celebration of heritage, hospitality, and the vibrant spirit of its people.
